Who I Am

Hi! I'm Samrudhi Manoj Naik, a first-year student of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering (EnTC) at MIT Academy of Engineering, Pune.

My creative side shows up in painting and crochet — crafts that taught me patience, precision, and attention to detail. I carry those same qualities into my engineering work.
